Frequently Asked Questions

+ What is DocEX?

DocEX is an AI-powered intelligent document processor designed to automate the extraction of information from various types of documents with high accuracy.

+ How does DocEX work?

DocEX uses advanced AI models to process and extract data from your documents. You simply upload your documents, and DocEX quickly delivers the extracted information in a structured format like JSON or Excel.

+ What types of documents can DocEX process?

DocEX can process a wide range of documents including invoices, contracts, receipts, forms, and more. It supports various formats like PDF, Word, and scanned images.

+ Is my data secure with DocEX?

Yes, security is a top priority for us. All documents are processed in a secure environment, and your data is protected with industry-standard encryption protocols.

+ Can DocEX integrate with my existing systems?

Absolutely! DocEX is designed to seamlessly integrate with your existing systems, making it easy to incorporate the extracted data into your workflows.

+ How can I get started with DocEX?

You can get started by booking a demo through our website or by directly trying out our platform for free. Our team is here to help you with any questions you may have during the setup process.

+ What are the pricing options for DocEX?

We offer flexible pricing plans to suit different business needs. You can choose from our basic, standard, and premium plans. For more details, please visit our pricing page or contact our sales team.

+ How accurate is the data extraction process?

DocEX achieves high accuracy in data extraction due to its advanced AI models. While results can vary depending on the document quality, our models are continuously trained to improve performance and accuracy.

+ Can DocEX handle multi-page documents?

Yes, DocEX is capable of processing multi-page documents. It can extract and consolidate data across all pages into a single structured output, making it easy to handle complex documents.

+ What support options are available?

We provide a range of support options, including online documentation, a customer support portal, and direct contact with our support team via email or phone. We also offer dedicated support for enterprise customers.

+ How does DocEX handle both digital and scanned documents with high accuracy?

DocEX leverages advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) models to process both digital and scanned documents with high precision. Whether you're dealing with clear digital text or complex scanned images, our OCR technology ensures accurate data extraction by effectively recognizing and interpreting text in various formats. This allows for reliable and efficient document processing across a wide range of document types.
